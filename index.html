About Us

Our Story

Zenith was formed by John Li in New York in 2013. As a pretty young company, we are different than others. First, we emphasize a lot on talents and teamwork, because we believe amazing can only happen when a group of talents know how to work together. Second, we embrace diversity. Our team members are from different backgrounds and cultures. Because of this, we are able to bring our services to different communities, and also allows us to understand the needs in different communities very well. Third, we brag on our professionalism. All of our team members are working for leading technology companies. We bring the cutting edge technologies along with best industry practices to our clients via Zenith. We put clients and quality of work in front of anything else.

Private sector is our main focus. We have noticed the technological needs have grown much faster in private sector than in public sector, and that is one of the reasons why Zenith was formed. We bridge the gap between business owners in private sector and nowadays technologies, so that those business owners can benefit from technologies that suit them best. With that being said, a deep understanding on what business owners really need, and mastery of technologies are the essential elements for the connection.

We treasure our clients, and grow with our clients. We manage to build a long term relationship with our clients, so we keep following up and providing free consultation to our clients even after the project has been delivered. We truly believe we are only as good as our clients are.

Characteristics

DDiversity is a key characteristic here in Zenith. Team members are from different backgrounds (most are bilingual), working in different industries, but all have a deep understanding on how to integrate business and technologies. We are not just pure computer engineers, but engineers who understand business.

TTalent is what we appreciate. Zenith is growing, and bringing more talented people on board. We believe when talent meets hardwork and teamwork, it is when amazing things happen. As a team, we share knowledge, help each other, and move forward together.

PProfessionalism is what our team members bring to work. We bring the industsy best practice to what we work in Zenith, which turns out to be a very beneficial thing for our clients. We bring our clients what industry-leading companies are using.
