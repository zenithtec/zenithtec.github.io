Privacy Policy for LetSmart Lite Application

Effective Date: April 20, 2025

This privacy policy describes how the LetSmart application ("the App") handles user data.

1. No User Account or Login Required:

The App does not require or offer user account creation or login functionality.

2. No Collection of Personal Data:

The App does not collect any personal data from its users. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (beyond what is necessary for basic app functionality as outlined by Apple's guidelines)
  • Usage data transmitted off the device

3. Local Data Storage:

All data related to your rental property management activities within the App (such as property details, tenant information, rent records, etc.) is stored locally on your device in a private SQLite database.

4. No Transmission of Data Off-Device:

The data stored within the App on your device is not transmitted to any external servers, third-party services, or the App developer. All data remains solely on your device.

5. No In-App Purchases:

The App does not offer any in-app purchases.

6. Third-Party Services:

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that would involve the collection or transmission of user data.

7. Children's Privacy:

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy:

As the App does not collect any data, significant changes to this policy are unlikely. However, in the event of any future updates that involve data handling, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly, and users will be informed through the App Store update notes.
